Menopause

"Menopause is the time that marks the end of your menstrual cycles. It's diagnosed after you've gone 12 months without a menstrual period."

Check out the link to find more about Menopause on the Mayo Clinic website. Mayo Clinic is a certified, top-rated hospital to help patients find answers to their questions.



Polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS)

"Polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal disorder common among women of reproductive age. Women with PCOS may have infrequent or prolonged menstrual periods or excess male hormone (androgen) levels."

Explore the Mayo Clinic website to learn more about what PCOS is, its symptoms, and treatments.



Premenstrual Dysphoric Disorder

"Premenstrual dysphoric disorder (PMDD) is a health problem that is similar to premenstrual syndrome (PMS) but is more serious. PMDD causes severe irritability, depression, or anxiety in the week or two before your period starts."

Take a look at the womenshealth.gov website to find the different between PMS and PMDD. Learn more about the symptoms, conditions, and ways to deal with this disorder.



Premenstrual Syndrome

"Premenstrual syndrome (PMS) has a wide variety of signs and symptoms, including mood swings, tender breasts, food cravings, fatigue, irritability and depression. It's estimated that as many as 3 of every 4 menstruating women have experienced some form of premenstrual syndrome."

Explore this website to learn more facts and statistics about this condition and how to treat it



Amenorrhea

"Amenorrhea (uh-men-o-REE-uh) is the absence of menstruation, often defined as missing one or more menstrual periods.""

Visit the Mayo Clinic website to learn more about different types of Amenorrhea, its causes, conditions, and additional information.



Endometriosis

"Endometriosis (en-doe-me-tree-O-sis) is an often painful disorder in which tissue similar to the tissue that normally lines the inside of your uterus — the endometrium — grows outside your uterus."

Take a look at the website linked above to find more about this condition
